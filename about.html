About EstateCraft

Discover our story, values, and commitment to excellence in luxury home building

Our Story

EstateCraft was founded in 1998 by architect Robert Davenport with a vision to redefine luxury home building. With over 25 years of experience, we have established ourselves as a premier builder of custom luxury homes, known for our exceptional craftsmanship, attention to detail, and commitment to client satisfaction.

Our philosophy is simple: every home should be a unique reflection of its owner's personality, lifestyle, and aspirations. We approach each project with fresh eyes, collaborating closely with our clients to bring their vision to life while incorporating innovative design solutions and sustainable building practices.

What sets us apart is our unwavering commitment to quality. We work with a select number of clients each year to ensure that every project receives the attention it deserves. Our team of architects, designers, and master craftsmen work in harmony to create homes that are not only beautiful but also functional, durable, and built to last for generations.

Our Approach

At EstateCraft, we believe that the process of building a luxury home should be as exceptional as the final result. Our comprehensive approach ensures a seamless experience from concept to completion.

Collaborative Design: We work closely with you to understand your vision, preferences, and lifestyle needs, creating a design that perfectly captures your dream home.

Meticulous Planning: Every detail is carefully considered and planned, from material selection to construction timelines, ensuring a smooth and efficient building process.

Quality Craftsmanship: Our team of skilled artisans and craftsmen take pride in their work, delivering exceptional quality in every aspect of your home.

Transparent Communication: We maintain open lines of communication throughout the project, keeping you informed and involved at every stage.

Our Values

The principles that guide everything we do

Excellence

We are committed to delivering the highest quality in every aspect of our work, from design to craftsmanship. Our attention to detail ensures that every home we build exceeds expectations.

Innovation

We embrace new technologies and design trends while respecting traditional craftsmanship. Our innovative approach allows us to create homes that are both timeless and contemporary.

Integrity

We believe in transparency, honesty, and building trust with our clients. Our relationships are built on mutual respect and a shared commitment to creating something extraordinary.
