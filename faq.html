Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to common questions about our luxury home building process

Common Questions

Get answers to the questions we hear most often from our clients

What is the typical timeline for a custom home build?

+

The timeline for a custom home build varies depending on the complexity and size of the project, but typically ranges from 12 to 24 months. This includes design development (2-4 months), permitting (1-3 months), and construction (9-17 months). We provide a detailed project timeline during the initial planning phase and maintain regular communication to keep you informed of progress.

Do you work with client-provided architects or designers?

+

Yes, we frequently collaborate with client-selected architects and designers. Our team has experience working with various design professionals and can seamlessly integrate into your existing project team. We also have our own in-house design team if you prefer a comprehensive solution. Our collaborative approach ensures that all aspects of your project are coordinated for the best possible outcome.

What sets EstateCraft apart from other luxury home builders?

+

Our distinction lies in our meticulous attention to detail, commitment to using premium materials, and our collaborative approach to design. We limit the number of projects we take on to ensure each client receives personalized attention. Additionally, our 25 years of experience has refined our processes to deliver exceptional quality and value. We also offer comprehensive services from design through construction, providing a seamless experience for our clients.

How do you handle budget management during the project?

+

We provide transparent, detailed cost estimates before construction begins and maintain open communication about any potential changes. Our project management system tracks expenses in real-time, and we provide regular budget updates. We also help clients make informed decisions when value engineering is needed to stay within budget while maintaining quality. Our goal is to deliver exceptional value while ensuring there are no surprises along the way.

What type of warranty do you offer on your homes?

+

We offer a comprehensive warranty package that includes a 10-year structural warranty, 2-year systems warranty (covering electrical, plumbing, and HVAC), and a 1-year craftsmanship warranty. Additionally, we provide a 90-day follow-up service after move-in to address any minor adjustments needed. Our commitment to quality doesn't end at completion - we maintain relationships with our clients long after they've moved into their new homes.

Can you work with challenging or unusual building sites?

+

Absolutely. We have extensive experience with challenging sites including steep slopes, narrow lots, and properties with unique environmental considerations. Our team includes specialists in geotechnical engineering, surveying, and environmental planning who can assess site conditions and develop appropriate solutions. We see challenging sites as opportunities to create unique, architecturally significant homes that maximize their settings.

How involved will I need to be during the construction process?

+

Your level of involvement is entirely up to you. We provide regular updates and involve clients in key decisions, but we also have systems in place to manage the project efficiently if you prefer a more hands-off approach. Most clients appreciate being involved in selections and key milestone reviews while trusting us to handle the day-to-day management. We're flexible and adapt our communication style to match your preferences.

Do you incorporate sustainable and energy-efficient features?

+

Yes, we strongly believe in building homes that are not only beautiful but also environmentally responsible. We offer a range of sustainable options including energy-efficient systems, sustainable materials, passive solar design, rainwater harvesting, and renewable energy integration. We can tailor these features to match your sustainability goals and budget, creating homes that are healthier, more comfortable, and have lower operating costs.
